About

Hey I'm Zack. I'm an enthusiastic and curious web developer and digital marketing professional based in the Los Angeles area who is passionate about technology, design, and business. I started my career doing digital marketing and web copywriting for LACED Agency, but I quickly discovered that I wanted to learn how to write websites themselves instead of just the copy that goes in them. I finally got my chance when I teamed up with some friends to launch Black Coffee LA, where I designed two websites and helped develop the company's marketing and sales strategy. In addition, I gained valuable direct sales experience selling coffee to consumers at various farmers' markets and live events.

I also work as a freelance developer and marketer, and am currently developing the website and marketing plan for Access 2 Senior Living, a new company that helps senior citizens locate assisted living and housing options free of charge. My increasing interest in technology eventually led me to General Assembly's 3-month Web Development Immersive course, where I learned to create apps that I previously would have thought were impossible or at least way ahead of my initial skill set. I am now looking to leverage my experience in marketing on the clienct, agency, and freelance sides with my newfound skills as a developer. I am seeking full-time or contract-to-hire work with a company where I can learn and grow my skillset as quickly as possible and utilize my abilities to add value.
